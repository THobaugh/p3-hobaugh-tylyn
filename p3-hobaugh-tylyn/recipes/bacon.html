Bacon Wrapped Stuffed Dates

Anything with bacon has my heart, and this quick appetizer is no different. It may sound like a daring mix of flavors, but trust me it is worth it!

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of Bacon
  • 1 container of pitted Dates
  • 1 package of Honey Goat Cheese
  • Toothpicks for each piece

Instructions:

  1. Slice the Dates on one side horizontally to create an opening.
  2. Using a small spoon, add some Honey Goat Cheese inside the Date.
  3. Cut each slice of Bacon in half vertically.
  4. Take one Honey Goat Cheese filled Date, and roll the Date into the Bacon./li>
  5. Place a Toothpick into the rolled Date, and place on a baking sheet covered pan.
  6. Cook at 350 degrees for 18-20 minutes, or until bacon has crisped.

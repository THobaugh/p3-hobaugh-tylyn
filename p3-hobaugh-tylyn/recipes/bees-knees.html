Beverages

Beverages | 12.09.20

Do you know how to make your favorite beverage?

My favorite beverage is the bees knees. Not only does it have such a fun and appealing name, but it mixes two of my favorite things, honey and alcohol. I discovered this beverage at a popular bar in college, and now anytime I see it on a menu I make sure to order it. Sometimes it is garnished with a honeycomb, and other times with a lemon peel, but either way it is a nice refreshing beverage. Now, did I ever think to make this for myself at home? Absolutely not, and I think it's because it was one of those things that just made it more special when I could go out and have it with some friends or family. However, since 2020 has been a crazy year for many of us. I decided it was time to learn how, and I highly recommend you learning how to make your own favorite beverage. Unlike my original belief, it doesn't take away the specialty of the beverage, it actually makes me appreciate the small differences that some bartenders make to the beverage.

Ingredients:

  • 3 shots of Gin
  • Half a Lemon
  • 1/2 cup of Honey Water
  • Lemon Peel or Honeycomb for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Put ice in a shaker, and add the Gin, Honey Water, and squeeze the Lemon.
  2. Shake ingredients until chilled.
  3. Pour into a cocktail glass, and garnish.

21+ Hot Cocoa

Not your child’s hot cocoa. This twist on the classic hot cocoa will warm you up without a doubt! It’s a perfect beverage to have to celebrate the holiday season, or have when you are in a chocolaty mood.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Hot Cocoa packet
  • 3/4 cup of Water or Milk
  • 1 shot of Baileys
  • 1/2 of Peppermint Schnapps
  • Optional: Marshmellows or Whipped Cream

Instructions:

  1. Warm up the Water or Milk in a mug.
  2. Once hot, add the Hot Cocoa packet and stir.
  3. Add the Baileys and Peppermint Schnapps and stir.
  4. Add your optional Marshmellows or Whipped Cream.

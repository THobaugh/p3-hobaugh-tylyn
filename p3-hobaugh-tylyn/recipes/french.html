It's time for some bubbles!

Itâ€™s time to add some bubbles to your beverage today! I would like to first start off by saying that I love gin, and when I was introduced to a gin and Champagne beverage I was sold. This is such a refreshing beverage that does not require a lot of ingredients, and is sure to please everyone. I recommend Hendricks gin, but you are more than welcome to use your favorite gin.

Ingredients:

  • 2 shots of Gin
  • 2 tbsp of Lemon Juice
  • 2 tbsp of Simple Sugar
  • 1 can of Sparkling Wine/Champagne

Instructions:

  1. Put ice in a shaker, and add the Gin, Lemon Juice, and Simple Sugar.
  2. Shake ingredients until chilled.
  3. Pour into a champagne glass until filled a little over halfway full.
  4. Top of the glass with Sparkling Wine/Champagne.

Holiday Martini

Beverages | 12.06.20

Easy holiday martini for your next get together.

Every holiday season should have a special beverage, and this winter is no different! This holiday martini is easy to make and only requires two items, if you don't want to garnish with fruit.

Ingredients:

  • 2 shots of Vodka
  • 1 cup of White Cranberry Juice
  • 1 bag of Cranberries to garnish

Instructions:

  1. Put ice in a shaker, and add the Vodka and White Cranberry Juice.
  2. Shake ingredients until chilled.
  3. Pour into a martini glass, and garnish with Cranberries.

Winter 2020

Winter 2020 | 12.05.20

Cozy up to a movie at your local drive-in.

Winter is by far my favorite season of the year. With the chill in the breeze, the gloomier weather, and the warm feeling you get all wrapped up in a cozy blanket; there is truly no other season that could make me happier. Depending on where you live, there could be a variety of options for activities to go out and enjoy. Tampa, FL is no different and this year a popular restaurant on the riverwalk opened up a drive in theater that is exactly what we needed. A large movie screen with spacing between cars allows everyone to be able to enjoy the movie without anyone being in your way. Also, having the city lights on the river as your backdrop is not a bad sight either. You can order from the concession stand, or enjoy one of the many food options inside Armature Works and bring it back to your car.

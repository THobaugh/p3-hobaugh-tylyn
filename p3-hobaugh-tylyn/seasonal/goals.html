Winter 2020

Winter 2020 | 12.07.20

Realistic new year goals.

Every New Year it feels like I make the same goals for myself, and then I either don't follow through or they never get off the ground. I have found that instead of making these resolutions at the beginning of the year, I should write down my goals when they come to mind, and then have an estimated time to complete that goal written beside it. Not only has this worked for me, but I've been able to cross them off the list and add more goals that I really did not see happening anytime soon. I think it takes the pressure off the beginning of the year, and the goals you write down throughout the year become more meaningful when you accomplish them! As the New Year approaches, it is a good time to reflect on the previous year, but instead of making goals right in the moment, take time to just appreciate the year that you had.

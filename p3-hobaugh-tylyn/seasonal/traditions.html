Winter 2020

Winter 2020 | 12.12.20

Holiday traditions galore!

I don't know about you, but my family have traditions galore during the holiday season! It is a fantastic way to get everyone in the family involved, and introduce traditions to some friends who may get involved. Below I have mentioned 3 traditions since there really are a decent amount to partake in each year.

One of the first traditions that we partake in, is the search for the ornament. I have a small immediate family, so we all go out to dinner and then go on a hunt for an ornament to add to the tree. Some people choose an ornament that represents something that reminds them of the year, and others choose an ornament that they just like. Another tradition my mom and I have is matching pajamas. Each year one person picks the pajamas, and it usually determines the theme of the wrapping paper. We wear them Christmas Eve, so when Christmas Day arrives and we open presents, we match for pictures. Speaking of Christmas Eve, in our family everyone can pick one present to open up that night, which has always added to the anticipation of opening the other presents the nex morning.

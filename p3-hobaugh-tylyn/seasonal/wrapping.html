Winter 2020 | 12.03.20

Wrapping up the holidays.

When it comes to wrapping presents, it is honestly one of my favorite things to do during the holiday season. Now I try not to go the total traditional route when it comes to my wrapping paper and ribbon. Adding a pop of color or pattern can really make your presents stand out under a Christmas tree, or at a gift exchange with your family and friends. Every year I have a color theme or an object as my theme, such as a pine cone. I know plenty of people who loathe wrapping their presents, and that is understandbable, but by adding fun colors and patterns this could make wrapping presents a little less tedious and fun for those who like to mix and match.
